BEST OF ALL

SAMOSA

The Beef Samosa, a culinary marvel rooted in diverse cultures, boasts a golden, crispy crust that encases a tantalizing filling. Tender beef, infused with a symphony of aromatic herbs and spices, creates a mouthwatering experience that leaves a lasting impression. This samosa is a testament to the artistry of culinary craftsmanship, blending textures and flavors in perfect harmony.

On the other hand, the Vegetarian Samosa is a celebration of nature's bounty and the endless possibilities of vegetarian cuisine. Wrapped in the same flaky crust, it reveals a vibrant medley of vegetables, such as potatoes, peas, and carrots, dancing alongside an array of aromatic spices. Each bite takes you on a journey of tastes, as the ingredients unite to create a sensational explosion on the palate.

DISCOVERY MENU

The wok, a versatile and iconic cooking vessel, holds the key to unlocking a multitude of culinary possibilities. It is a symbol of tradition and innovation, hailing from the depths of Asian kitchens and now embraced by chefs around the globe. With its high heat, curved shape, and rapid cooking method, the wok allows ingredients to be seared, stir-fried, and sautéed to perfection, preserving their natural flavors and creating mouthwatering combinations.

As you peruse our menu, you will discover a tantalizing array of wok-inspired dishes that showcase the mastery of our chefs. From sizzling stir-fries to aromatic curries, each dish pays homage to the centuries-old tradition of wok cooking while embracing modern culinary techniques and creative interpretations.
